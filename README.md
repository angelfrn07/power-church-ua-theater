## The deal in one paragraph

Per LOI dated 5/3/2026: Power Church purchases the 83,422 sqft former UA theater from Cinergy 110 LLC for **$3.8M**. 20% down ($760K from a LOC against the existing SE 10th Ave church property), $3.04M conventional commercial mortgage from ANB at 7%/25-yr. Initial renovation $100K-$500K from cash flow. The modeler also includes a **Strategic Counter** scenario ($2M to Cinergy + $2M bank construction loan) and a **Master Lease** fallback option.
